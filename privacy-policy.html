Introduction

At Kolabix ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or engage with us.

Our Commitment

"We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information with any third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purposes."

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Company name and business information
  • Project requirements and communication content
  • Billing and payment information (processed securely)

Usage Information:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on website
  • Referring website and search terms used
  • Device type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery:

  • Provide and deliver our services
  • Communicate about projects

Website Improvement:

  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

  • Optimize website performance

Information Sharing and Disclosure

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes. Your data stays with us and is used solely for providing our services to you.

Limited Exceptions:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: Essential service providers (hosting, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
